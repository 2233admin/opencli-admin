# Constrain Break Glass to service restoration

Break Glass is a 15-minute Admin-initiated path for pre-registered service-restoration actions, not a general permission bypass. It may skip ordinary approval waiting during an incident but still passes through Risk Policy, Gates, the Actuator, and evidence capture; sensitive policy, identity, credential, egress, and deletion changes remain forbidden, and another Admin—or a Platform Admin entering through recorded Workspace access—must review its use.
