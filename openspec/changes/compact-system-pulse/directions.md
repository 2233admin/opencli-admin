# Directions

## A — Neutral instrument strip (selected)

White clock and labels, compact telemetry at left, state color restricted to the status label and exceptional metrics. Best scanability and least visual noise.

## B — State-tinted strip

Tint the entire strip green/amber by health. Stronger signal, but creates color fatigue and overstates normal operation.

Decision: A. Operational dashboards should stay quiet until intervention is needed.
